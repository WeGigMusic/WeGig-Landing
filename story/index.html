The story behind WeGig

WeGig started from a simple idea. Live music is one of the most meaningful parts of our lives, but the memories don’t live anywhere outside our minds.

We track workouts and places we’ve been, which is great but gigs often disappear into discarded tickets, camera rolls and half-forgotten nights.

A gig is more than an event. It is where you discover artists, mark moments in your life and share experiences.

But there’s no real home for that history.

WeGig is being built to change that.

What WeGig is

WeGig is a place to track the gigs you’ve been to, relive the moments that mattered and discover what’s next.

Over time, it will become your personal live music history, a way to discover gigs and a new layer of social connection around music.

Community input

WeGig is being built step by step, with feedback shaping what comes next.

If there’s something you want to see, suggest it.

If something doesn’t work, shout.

This is just the beginning.
