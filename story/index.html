The story behind WeGig

WeGig started from a simple idea. Live music is a huge part of our lives but the memories don’t really live anywhere.

We track workouts, places we’ve been and all sorts of things but gigs end up scattered across tickets, camera rolls and half-forgotten nights.

A gig is more than just an event. It’s where you discover artists, mark moments in your life and share experiences. But there’s no real home for that.

So we’re building one.

What WeGig is

WeGig is a place to track the gigs you’ve been to and relive the ones that mattered.

Over time it’ll become your personal live music history and a way to discover what’s next.

Built with you

WeGig is being built step by step, shaped by real feedback.

If there’s something you want, suggest it. If something doesn’t work, shout.

This is just the beginning.
