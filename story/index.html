The story behind WeGig

WeGig started from a simple idea: live music is one of the most meaningful parts of people’s lives, but the memories don’t live anywhere properly.

We track films, books, workouts, and places we’ve been — but gigs often disappear into old tickets, camera rolls, and half-forgotten nights.

Why it matters

A gig is more than an event. It is where you discover artists, mark moments in your life, and share experiences with friends.

But there’s no real home for that history.

WeGig is being built to change that.

What WeGig is

WeGig is a place to track the gigs you’ve been to, relive the moments that mattered, and discover what’s next.

Over time, it will become:

  • Your personal live music history
  • A way to discover gigs through friends
  • A new layer of social connection around music

Built in public

WeGig is being built step by step, with feedback shaping what comes next.

If there’s something you want to see, suggest it.

If something doesn’t work, say it.

This is just the beginning.
